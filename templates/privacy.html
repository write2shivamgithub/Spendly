Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide when you create an account (such as your name and email address) and the expense data you enter while using Spendly, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service — to display your expenses, generate summaries, and personalise your experience. We do not use your financial data for advertising or sell it to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third-party services for their own purposes. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) that process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you at any time. You may delete your account from within the app, which will permanently remove your data from our systems.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information provided on our website.
